How Address Lookup Works

We convert your address to coordinates, then find nearby outage events using a weighted algorithm that considers both distance and outage size.

The Process

  1. Geocoding: Convert your address to GPS coordinates using OpenStreetMap
  2. Distance calculation: Find all outages within 0.6 miles of your location
  3. Smart ranking: Weight by both distance AND outage size (larger outages affecting more customers cover wider areas)

Why might it be wrong?

  • Two neighbors can be on different electrical equipment and have different outages
  • Outage boundaries don't follow street lines
  • Multiple small outages may be near you

Accuracy

~85-95% for the top suggestion. We show you the top 3 matches so you can confirm which one is yours.

Tip: If the top result doesn't match what you see on the NES map, try #2 or #3, or verify on the NES Outage Map.
